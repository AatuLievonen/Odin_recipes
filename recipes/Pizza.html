Pizza recipe

Pizza

pizza is a wonderful food for the whole family. From children to grandparents, everybody loves it.

Ingredients

  • tomato paste
  • cheese
  • tuna
  • pizza dough

Steps

  1. First flatten the pizza dough
  2. Then spread the tomato paste evenly across the pizza
  3. Sprinkle cheese on the pizza
  4. Add tuna on top of the pizza
  5. Bake the pizza for 15min in 225 degrees
  6. Enjoy.
